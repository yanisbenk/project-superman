Who is Superman?

Superman is a fictional superhero created by writer Jerry Siegel and artist Joe Shuster. He first appeared in Action Comics #1 in 1938 and has since become one of the most iconic and recognizable characters in popular culture.

Superman's powers

Superman possesses a wide range of powers, including superhuman strength, speed, and durability, as well as flight, heat vision, and x-ray vision. He is also virtually indestructible, with his only known weakness being exposure to Kryptonite.

Superman in popular culture

Superman has appeared in countless comics, movies, TV shows, and other media over the years. He has been portrayed by many different actors, including Christopher Reeve, Henry Cavill, and Tyler Hoechlin. Superman has also inspired numerous other superheroes and has become a symbol of hope and justice for many people around the world.
